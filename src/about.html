Mike's Automotives

For over three decades, our dealership, Mike's Automotives, has been an unwavering pillar in the community, proudly serving with dedication and commitment. With a legacy that spans more than 30 years, we have become a trusted automotive partner, fostering lasting connections with our community. Over this time, we are thrilled to have facilitated the sale of over 10,000 cars, each transaction representing a chapter in our story of customer satisfaction. Our success is not just measured in numbers; it is woven into the fabric of the community we cherish.

Behind this achievement is a team of over 50 dedicated employees, including skilled salespersons, expert mechanics, and knowledgeable associates. Together, we form a dynamic and passionate workforce committed to delivering an exceptional automotive experience. At Mike's Automotives, we take pride in our role as a community-driven dealership, and we eagerly anticipate the opportunity to continue serving you with integrity, quality vehicles, and a team that is second to none. Here's to another 30 years of trust, service, and excellence!
